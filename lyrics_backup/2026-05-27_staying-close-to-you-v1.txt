[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the fire glowing when you look my way
Got a midnight kind of secret that I want to say
Let the shadows wrap around us while the embers burn
I am staying close to you, waiting for my turn

[Verse 1]
There is a whisper in the wind across the wooden floor
Every time you step a little closer to my door
I have been counting every second since the sun went down
Just to have you to myself, far away from town

[Pre-Chorus]
The room is quiet but my heart is beating like a drum
I see the promise in your eyes of everything to come
Pulling down the curtains, letting out a heavy sigh
I am lost inside the magic of the starry sky

[Chorus]
Staying close to you, where the whiskey meets the ice
Staying close to you, yeah, it feels like paradise
I can taste the sweet temptation on your painted lips
Staying close to you, through the velvet of your kiss

[Post-Chorus]
(Staying close to you)
Never going to let this moment fade away

[Verse 2]

We are speaking in a language only we can understand
